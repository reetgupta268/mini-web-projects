My Review of Dear Zindagi

By Reet Gupta | January 30, 2026

=

Movie Poster

Plot

Dear Zindagi tells the story of Kaira, a young cinematographer struggling with relationships, career pressure, and emotional baggage. The film explores mental health in a gentle and relatable way, showing that it’s okay to seek help and take time to understand yourself.

Characters

Alia Bhatt delivers a natural and heartfelt performance as Kaira. Shah Rukh Khan, as Dr. Jehangir Khan, plays a calm and thoughtful therapist who guides her without judgment. The supporting characters feel realistic and add depth to the narrative.

Visuals

The visuals are soothing and beautifully shot, especially the scenes set in Goa. Soft colors, natural lighting, and peaceful locations perfectly match the film’s emotional tone and reflective mood.

“How you treat yourself sets the standard for others.”

My Verdict

Dear Zindagi is a warm, comforting film that feels like a conversation rather than a lecture. It encourages self-love, emotional honesty, and mental well-being. You can learn more about the movie here.
