Sunday Morning Masala Dosa

Masala dosa is more than just a breakfast dish. It is a South Indian classic often prepared on calm Sunday mornings. The crispy texture and spicy potato filling make it comforting and satisfying.

Ingredients

  • Dosa batter
  • Boiled potatoes
  • Onions
  • Green chilies
  • Mustard seeds
  • Curry leaves
  • Salt
  • Oil or ghee

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds. Wait until they crackle.
  2. Add onions, green chilies, and curry leaves. Sauté until golden.
  3. Add mashed potatoes and salt. Mix well and cook for 2–3 minutes.
  4. Heat a flat pan and spread the dosa batter in a circular motion.
  5. Drizzle oil around the edges and cook until crispy. Do not flip too early.
  6. Place the potato filling in the center and fold the dosa. Serve hot.

Serve with coconut chutney and sambar for the best experience.
